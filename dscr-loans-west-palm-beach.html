Palm Beach County's growing investment market. Finance rental properties based on cash flow — not your tax returns or personal income.

West Palm Beach and Palm Beach County represent one of the most compelling real estate investment opportunities in South Florida. Sandwiched between the ultra-premium Palm Beach island market and the more active Fort Lauderdale/Miami corridor, West Palm Beach offers investors meaningful appreciation potential, strong rental fundamentals, and more accessible price points than its neighbors to the south. DSCR loans are how most serious Palm Beach County investors finance — qualifying on rental income without W-2s or tax returns.

Why Palm Beach County Attracts Investors

Palm Beach County has several structural investment advantages:

  • Financial services migration — Significant financial firms have relocated to Palm Beach County from New York, driving high-income professional rental demand for premium properties.
  • More affordable than Palm Beach Island — West Palm Beach proper and western Palm Beach County suburbs offer investment-grade properties at more accessible price points than the island.
  • Strong rental demand — Growing permanent population, seasonal residents, and professional renters create year-round rental demand.
  • Lower insurance vs Miami — Palm Beach County insurance costs, while elevated, are generally lower than Miami-Dade for comparable inland properties.

Palm Beach County Submarkets

Key investment submarkets in Palm Beach County:

  • West Palm Beach city — Strong rental demand, revitalized downtown, good mix of price points for investors.
  • Lake Worth Beach / Lantana — More affordable coastal-adjacent markets with strong rental demand.
  • Boynton Beach / Delray Beach — Active rental markets, more diverse price points, strong demand from Boca Raton spillover.
  • Wellington — Equestrian community with unique seasonal rental demand from horse show participants and families.
  • Boca Raton — Premium market, higher acquisition costs, but premium rental rates and strong appreciation track record.
  • Royal Palm Beach / Greenacres — Inland western suburbs with more affordable price points and stronger DSCR ratios.

DSCR Loan Requirements for West Palm Beach

Standard Florida DSCR guidelines apply in Palm Beach County:

  • Minimum credit score: 620 (660+ recommended)
  • Minimum DSCR: 1.0
  • Down payment: 20–25% for purchase
  • LLC/entity vesting: accepted
  • No W-2s or tax returns required
  • STR programs available for properly licensed short-term rentals
  • Loan amounts: $100,000 to $3M+
