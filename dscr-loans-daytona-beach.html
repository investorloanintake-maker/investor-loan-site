DSCR Loans in Daytona Beach

Volusia County's underrated investment market. More affordable than Orlando or Tampa, with beach STR potential and strong year-round rental demand.

Viador Partners, NMLS #2822744 20 Years Lending Experience Viador Partners LLC

Daytona Beach and Volusia County sit between Orlando and Jacksonville on Florida's east coast, offering investors a distinct market profile: more affordable acquisition prices than either metro, a significant beach tourism STR market, a growing retiree and permanent resident population, and Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University creating specialized professional rental demand. DSCR loans are how most Daytona Beach area investors finance — qualifying on rental income without W-2s or tax returns.

The Daytona Beach Investment Market

Daytona Beach and Volusia County offer investors several distinct advantages:

  • More affordable than core Florida metros — Daytona Beach and inland Volusia County offer more accessible acquisition prices than Orlando or Tampa Bay, producing stronger DSCR ratios at comparable rental rates.
  • Beach STR market — Daytona Beach has historically strong Spring Break and motorsports event STR demand (Daytona 500, Bike Week). Year-round beach tourism adds baseline STR income.
  • Embry-Riddle — One of the world's premier aviation universities creates a specialized professional and international student rental market in the Daytona Beach area.
  • Retiree and snowbird market — Volusia County attracts significant retiree in-migration, driving strong year-round long-term rental demand for lower-maintenance properties.
  • I-4 Corridor growth — The corridor between Daytona and Orlando continues to attract development and employment, improving Volusia County's long-term investment fundamentals.

Daytona Beach Submarkets

Key Volusia County investment submarkets:

  • Daytona Beach Shores / Ormond Beach — Premium beachfront STR market. Higher prices, strong seasonal income.
  • Port Orange / South Daytona — More affordable suburban market, strong family and professional rental demand. Better DSCR ratios than beachfront.
  • DeLand — Inland Volusia, college town atmosphere, most affordable entry points in the county, strong cap rates.
  • New Smyrna Beach — Premium STR market south of Daytona, attracting Airbnb investors seeking Surf Capital of the East Coast branding.
  • Deltona — Fast-growing bedroom community, strong family rental demand, highly accessible prices.

DSCR Financing for Daytona Beach

Standard Florida DSCR guidelines apply in Volusia County:

  • Minimum credit score: 620 (660+ recommended)
  • Minimum DSCR: 1.0
  • Down payment: 20-25% for purchase
  • STR programs available for beach properties with documented income or market appraisal
  • LLC vesting: accepted
  • Loan amounts: $100,000 to $3M+
