DSCR Loans · Cleveland, Ohio

DSCR Loans in Cleveland, Ohio

One of the highest cap rate markets in the Midwest. Strong cash flow, affordable entry, and DSCR ratios that make financing straightforward.

Viador Partners, NMLS #2822744 20 Years Lending Experience Florida & Ohio

Cleveland offers some of the most compelling investment property fundamentals of any major US metro. Low acquisition costs combined with strong rental demand from a large working-class population — anchored by healthcare (Cleveland Clinic, University Hospitals), manufacturing, and logistics — produce cap rates and DSCR ratios that are increasingly rare in 2026's market environment.

Cleveland Investment Property Fundamentals

What makes Cleveland stand out for DSCR investors:

8–12%Typical cap rates
$120KMedian SFR price
1.4+Typical DSCR ratio

A $120,000 Cleveland rental purchased with 25% down might carry a PITIA of $850/month. Rented at $1,250/month, that produces a DSCR of 1.47 — strong enough to qualify for the best DSCR rate pricing. These metrics are increasingly hard to find in coastal markets.

Cleveland Investment Submarkets

Cleveland has diverse investment submarkets with different risk/return profiles:

  • Lakewood: Stable, working-class neighborhood, consistent rental demand, moderate cap rates
  • Euclid / South Euclid: High cap rates, strong cash flow, improving neighborhood fundamentals
  • Parma / Parma Heights: Suburban Cleveland, family rental demand, stable values
  • Mentor / Willoughby: Eastern suburbs, professional rental market
  • Bedford / Maple Heights: Highest cap rates in the metro, strong yield investors
  • University Circle: Hospital district, strong professional rental demand

Cleveland DSCR Loan Requirements

Standard Ohio DSCR guidelines apply to Cleveland properties:

  • Minimum credit score: 620
  • Minimum DSCR: 1.0 (Cleveland properties typically produce 1.3–1.5+)
  • Down payment: 20–25%
  • LLC vesting: accepted
  • Loan amounts: $75,000 minimum for most programs (Cleveland's lower price points can be a constraint)

Minimum Loan Amount Note

Some DSCR lenders have minimum loan amounts of $100,000 or higher, which can be a constraint in Cleveland where purchase prices are often $75,000–$120,000. Viador Partners works with lenders with lower minimum loan amounts for Ohio markets. Submit your deal to discuss.
