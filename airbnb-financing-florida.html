Airbnb Financing -- Florida

Airbnb Financing in Florida

From your first vacation rental to a portfolio of STR properties. Finance based on rental income -- not your tax returns.

Viador Partners, NMLS #2822744 20 Years Lending Experience Florida & Ohio

Airbnb investing in Florida has become one of the most common wealth-building strategies for both experienced real estate investors and first-time vacation rental owners. The challenge is financing. Conventional lenders either refuse to finance properties used as short-term rentals or require income documentation that most Airbnb investors cannot provide. DSCR loans solve both problems.

Conventional vs DSCR for Airbnb Properties

Most Airbnb investors discover the conventional lending wall quickly:

  • Conventional lenders typically require that a property be used as a long-term rental or primary residence
  • Some conventional lenders allow STR but require the property to be owner-occupied part of the year
  • Fannie Mae guidelines for investment properties require full income documentation
  • Self-employed Airbnb owners often show tax returns that do not reflect actual STR cash flow

DSCR loans solve all of these problems. They are specifically designed for investment properties, accept STR income, do not require income documentation, and can close in an LLC.

How to Calculate DSCR for Your Florida Airbnb

DSCR for an Airbnb property is calculated the same way as any rental -- monthly gross rent divided by monthly PITIA. The key is which rent figure to use:

  1. Option 1: 12-Month STR Income Average

    Take your last 12 months of gross Airbnb revenue (before platform fees and expenses). Divide by 12 to get monthly gross STR income. Use this as the numerator in your DSCR calculation.

  2. Option 2: Market Rent Appraisal

    The appraiser evaluates what the property would rent for as a long-term rental. This is used as the denominator. Often more conservative than actual STR income, but provides a stable baseline.

  3. Calculate DSCR

    Monthly rent divided by PITIA. If result is 1.0 or higher, you likely qualify. Use our free DSCR calculator at viadorpartners.com/dscr-calculator to run your numbers.

Florida Airbnb Financing Requirements

To qualify for Airbnb DSCR financing in Florida:

  • Credit score: 620 minimum, 680+ for best pricing
  • Down payment: 25% for STR properties (vs. 20% for LTR)
  • DSCR: 1.0 minimum based on STR income or market rent
  • HOA clearance: Written confirmation or CC&R review confirming STR is permitted
  • Zoning: Local municipality must permit STR in property location
  • LLC vesting: Available and recommended for liability protection
  • Loan amounts: $100K to $3M+

First-Time Florida Airbnb Investor?

If you do not yet have 12 months of STR income, use the market rent approach. Many lenders prefer this anyway because it produces a stable DSCR calculation. Purchase the property, establish STR operations, and on your next refinance or acquisition you can use documented STR income instead.
