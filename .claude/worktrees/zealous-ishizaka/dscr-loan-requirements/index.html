FAQs

What are the minimum requirements?
1.0x DSCR, 620+ credit, 20-25% down, non-owner occupied. No W-2 or tax returns.
What documents do I need?
Address, lease, insurance quote, credit auth, LLC docs if applicable. No tax returns or bank statements.
What credit score?
620 minimum. 720+ for best rates. 679 to 720 saves $75-150/month on $300K.
How much down?
20% minimum. 25% at DSCR 1.0-1.09x.
Can I use LLC?
Yes — most lenders allow LLC vesting.
