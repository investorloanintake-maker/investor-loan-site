How It Works

How SBA Franchise Financing Works

01

SBA 7(a) is the primary vehicle — up to $5M in total financing for franchise acquisition and buildout.

02

Pre-approved franchises (SBA Franchise Directory) close in 60–90 days with streamlined documentation.

03

Non-approved franchises require additional review — typical timeline is 90–120 days to close.

04

Typical structure: 10% borrower equity, 90% SBA-guaranteed loan — one of the lowest down payments in commercial lending.

05

Covers: franchise fee, equipment, leasehold improvements, working capital — one loan for the full launch.
