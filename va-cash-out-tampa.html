The Strategy

How the Two-Loan Strategy Actually Works

01

Use your VA loan to access equity

From your primary residence. This replaces your current mortgage and must remain your primary home.

02

Use that capital as a down payment

On a rental property. Your extracted equity funds the acquisition.

03

Finance the rental with a DSCR loan

Approved based on the property's income — not your W2. LLC eligible.

These are two separate loans. The rental does NOT use your VA eligibility.

What Most Veterans Don't Know

Yes — Some Veterans Have More Than One VA Loan

If you have remaining VA entitlement, you may be able to:

• Keep your current home and convert it to a rental
• Use a VA loan again for your next primary residence
• Access equity from retained properties through investor financing
Eligibility depends on entitlement, loan limits, and occupancy requirements. We review this case-by-case — no assumptions.
