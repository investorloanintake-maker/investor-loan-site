Common Questions

Straight Answers

My interest rate is low — why would I refinance?
We only recommend a VA cash-out when the full math works in your favor. We model the complete scenario — new payment, equity deployed, rental cash flow — before you commit to anything. If it doesn't pencil, we tell you not to do it.
Can I really qualify for two loans?
Yes — structured correctly. The VA cash-out is on your primary residence. The DSCR loan is on the investment property and qualifies on rental income, not your personal income or W2. LLCs are eligible in Florida.
Is this risky?
Every deal goes through viascore.ai before we recommend it. If the numbers don't support positive cash flow, we tell you. We're not in the business of closing deals that don't make sense for you long-term.
