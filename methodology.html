Methodology

Maintained by Chad Evers, Mortgage Loan Originator, NMLS #2822744 · Last reviewed: June 7, 2026

Primary sources first

We research from official program and regulatory sources before secondary commentary, and we link the primary source on the page. See our source library.

How figures are presented

Where rules vary by state or county, we present ranges and direct readers to the authoritative local source rather than stating a single national figure, with a “confirm locally” note.

What we do not do

We do not make individualized eligibility determinations online, and we do not quote rates, payments, or savings. Viador Partners is an advisory and is not the lender.

Tools and calculators

Calculators provide educational estimates with stated assumptions. They are not a Loan Estimate, an offer, or a commitment to lend; actual terms are set by the lender and program.
