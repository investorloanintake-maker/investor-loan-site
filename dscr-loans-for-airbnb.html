DSCR Loans · Short-Term Rentals

DSCR Loans for Airbnb & STR Properties

Finance short-term rental properties based on what they actually earn — not what a long-term lease would generate. STR-specific DSCR programs are now widely available.

Chad Evers, NMLS #2822744 20 Years Lending Experience Viador Partners LLC

Short-term rental investing has created a category of real estate investors that conventional lending was never designed to serve. An Airbnb property generating $4,800/month gross revenue looks nothing like a property with a $1,800/month long-term lease — and until recently, most lenders valued it exactly the same way. DSCR loans for short-term rentals have evolved to close that gap, using documented STR income or market-rate STR appraisals to underwrite the deal the way it actually works.

How STR DSCR Loans Work

Standard DSCR loans use long-term market rent from an appraisal to calculate the debt service coverage ratio. STR DSCR loans add a second option: using documented short-term rental income from platforms like Airbnb, VRBO, and Furnished Finder.

There are two income documentation approaches for STR DSCR loans:

  • Documented STR income — 12 months of platform income statements (Airbnb payout reports, VRBO earnings summaries). The lender applies a vacancy factor and uses the net figure for DSCR calculation. Requires an existing operating history.
  • STR market rent appraisal — A specialized appraiser analyzes comparable short-term rental properties in the area and provides a market STR income estimate. Used for new purchases or properties without 12-month history. Not all appraisers offer this service.

Which STR Markets Have Active DSCR Lenders

Not all markets have sufficient comparable STR data for appraisers to support STR income methodology. Markets with established STR DSCR lending activity include:

  • Florida — Tampa Bay, Orlando/Kissimmee, Panama City Beach, Naples/Fort Myers, Key West corridor, Miami Beach, Destin/30A
  • Ohio — Columbus (university market), lakefront properties on Lake Erie
  • Tennessee — Smoky Mountains, Nashville, Gatlinburg
  • Texas — Austin, San Antonio, Gulf Coast
  • Arizona — Scottsdale, Sedona, Phoenix metro
  • Colorado — Denver, mountain resort markets

Markets with limited STR comps — rural areas, smaller metros — may require using long-term market rent for DSCR calculation, potentially undervaluing the actual income potential.

STR DSCR Loan Requirements

STR DSCR programs typically have slightly tighter requirements than standard DSCR loans:

  • Minimum credit score: 660 (higher than standard 620 on some STR programs)
  • Minimum DSCR: 1.0 using STR income methodology
  • Down payment: 25–30% (higher than standard 20–25%)
  • Maximum LTV: 75% on purchase, 70% on cash-out
  • Income documentation: 12 months platform statements OR STR market appraisal
  • Property type: 1–4 unit, must be zoning-compliant for STR use
  • HOA/condo: must verify STR is permitted by HOA

Critical STR Due Diligence

Before financing an Airbnb investment, verify: (1) Local zoning permits short-term rental use, (2) HOA rules do not restrict rentals under 30 days, (3) The property is registered or registerable under local STR licensing requirements. Regulations change frequently in popular markets — Tampa, Jacksonville, and many Florida beach communities have added restrictions in recent years.

STR vs Long-Term Rental DSCR Comparison

Whether to underwrite as STR or long-term rental depends on the market and the property:

  • If STR income is 2x+ long-term market rent: STR underwriting almost always wins
  • If STR income is 1.2–1.5x long-term rent: run both scenarios; lender fees and rate premium may favor long-term underwriting
  • If STR income is under 12 months documented: long-term market rent may be your only option at close; refinance later using actual STR history
  • If the market has STR restrictions: long-term underwriting is safer and protects against regulatory risk
