Fort Lauderdale and Broward County sit between Miami's high prices and Palm Beach's premium market, offering investors a more accessible entry point into South Florida real estate with strong rental fundamentals. A growing population, major employment base, and robust short-term rental demand near the beach make Broward County one of the most active investor markets in Florida. DSCR loans are the primary financing tool — qualifying on rental income without the personal income documentation hurdles of conventional lending.

Fort Lauderdale DSCR Loan Specifics

Financing investment properties in Fort Lauderdale has some South Florida-specific considerations:

  • Insurance — South Florida insurance costs are elevated but generally lower than coastal Tampa Bay and Gulf Coast markets. Budget $200–$400/month for insurance on a $400,000 Fort Lauderdale investment property.
  • Condo market — Fort Lauderdale has significant condo inventory. DSCR condo financing requires lender condo approval. Post-Surfside legislation has added inspection and reserve requirements affecting some older South Florida condo buildings.
  • STR municipal rules — Fort Lauderdale has adopted STR licensing requirements. Hollywood and Deerfield Beach have different rules. Verify current regulations before purchasing for short-term rental purposes.
  • Flood zones — Significant portions of coastal and near-coastal Broward are in FEMA flood zones. Flood insurance adds to PITIA and can compress DSCR on marginal deals.

Broward County Submarkets

Key investment submarkets in Broward County:

  • Fort Lauderdale Beach / Victoria Park — Premium STR and long-term rental demand. Higher price points, strong rents.
  • Hollywood / Hallandale Beach — More affordable than Fort Lauderdale proper, strong rental demand, active investor market.
  • Pompano Beach / Deerfield Beach — Value-add opportunities, improving market, lower entry points with growing rental demand.
  • Coral Springs / Plantation — Inland Broward family rental market. Strong tenant quality, stable demand, lower insurance costs than coastal.
  • Miramar / Pembroke Pines — South Broward working-family rental market. Strong employment base from healthcare and public sector.
