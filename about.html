The Story

Built from the inside out.

I spent 17 years inside the system — SVP at Congressional Bank, SVP at BayFirst, VP at Citi. I know exactly how those institutions think, what they'll approve, and more importantly, what they'll never approve.

The investors I kept seeing turned away weren't bad borrowers. They were buying in LLCs. They were self-employed. Their income didn't fit a W-2 box. The property made sense — the paperwork didn't conform.

Business purpose lending exists specifically for those deals. Most investors don't know it exists. Most realtors have never heard of it. That gap is exactly where 46&2 Partners operates.

How I Work

Deal first. Always.

I don't start with a credit pull. I start with the property, the strategy, and the numbers. If the deal makes sense, we find the path. If it doesn't, I'll tell you why — and what would need to change.

My lender relationships are direct — not a marketplace, not a broker chain. That's what allows 10-day closes. That's what allows flexibility on deal structure that a bank portal never could.
