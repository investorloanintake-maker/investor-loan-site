Programs

More options. One clear recommendation.

DSCR Loans

Rental-income-based financing for long-term and short-term rentals. No W-2s or tax returns required. LLC-vestable.

Investor Cash-Out

Refinance options for investors looking to access equity in existing properties. DSCR-based and conventional paths available.

Bridge / Fix & Flip

Short-term capital for acquisition, rehab, and transition strategies. Often paired with a DSCR takeout refinance at stabilization.

Bank Statement

Alternative documentation for self-employed investors. 12–24 months of deposits used in place of tax returns.

Foreign National / ITIN

Financing paths for eligible non-U.S. or alternative-documentation borrowers investing in U.S. real property.

Portfolio / LLC

Entity and portfolio-oriented structures for investors scaling multiple properties under a single note or blanket loan.

Common situations

Real problems. Real financing paths.

The rent almost works

DSCR looks close, but taxes, insurance, HOA, or payment assumptions don't pencil. There may be a path — or a structure adjustment that makes it work.

The down payment is short

We review equity, partner capital, bridge options, seller structure, or a future acquisition plan — not just whether you have 25% today.

The tax returns don't tell the full story

A self-employed investor may need bank-statement, DSCR, or alternative documentation. Write-offs that reduce taxable income don't have to sink the deal.

The property needs a transition plan

A BRRRR or rehab project may need short-term capital first, then a DSCR takeout later. We can review the full sequence, not just the first loan.
