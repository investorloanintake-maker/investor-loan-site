⚡ Quick Answer
Ohio DSCR loans qualify on investment property rental income — no W-2, no tax returns required. DSCR = monthly rent ÷ monthly PITIA. Ohio's strongest DSCR markets are Akron and Dayton — cap rates of 7.5–11% produce DSCR ratios of 1.15–1.40x at 20% down. Ohio's critical gotcha: county tax rates vary by 2x — Cuyahoga County (Cleveland) at 2.1–2.5% vs Franklin County (Columbus) at 1.1–1.4%. Always use the specific county rate.
