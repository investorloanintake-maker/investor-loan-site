DSCR Quick Check

Enter rent and payment to estimate your DSCR.

Why this matters

DSCR — Debt Service Coverage Ratio — measures whether the property's rental income covers its loan payment. A DSCR of 1.0 means rent equals the payment; above 1.25 is typically preferred by lenders.

Some investor loan programs rely more on property cash flow than traditional income documentation, making DSCR a key qualifying metric for rental properties.
