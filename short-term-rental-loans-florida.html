Short-Term Rental Loans in Florida

Finance your Airbnb or vacation rental without W-2s or tax returns. DSCR programs that understand the STR market.

Viador Partners, NMLS #2822744 20 Years Lending Experience Florida & Ohio

Florida is the number one short-term rental state in the country by volume. Kissimmee, Orlando, Miami Beach, Panama City Beach, Anna Maria Island, and Destin generate some of the highest short-term rental revenues in the United States. DSCR loans designed for STR investors make financing these properties possible without the income documentation headaches that make conventional lending unworkable for most vacation rental owners.

How STR DSCR Loans Work

Short-term rental DSCR loans work like standard DSCR loans with one key difference: rental income is calculated based on short-term rental income rather than long-term market rent.

Lenders use one of two income approaches for Florida STR properties:

  • Documented STR income: 12 months of Airbnb, VRBO, or property management statements showing actual gross revenue. Lenders typically apply a vacancy and expense factor to arrive at qualifying income.
  • Market rent approach: For properties without 12 months of STR history, the appraiser provides a market rent opinion based on comparable long-term rentals. Many STR investors prefer this as it often qualifies at a higher effective DSCR than seasonal STR averages.

Florida STR Markets by Revenue Potential

Florida STR markets vary significantly by revenue potential and regulatory environment:

  • Kissimmee / Osceola County: Highest STR volume in the state. Disney proximity drives year-round demand. STR-friendly regulations. Typical homes generate $40,000-$100,000+ annually.
  • Orlando / Orange County: Theme park proximity with stricter STR ordinances. Verify zoning before purchasing.
  • Panama City Beach: Strong seasonal beach market. Summer peaks are very high, winter is softer. Budget for seasonal variance.
  • Destin / Fort Walton Beach: Premium beach market. High ADR, strong summer demand.
  • Anna Maria Island / Bradenton Beach: High-end STR market with strong year-round demand.
  • Miami / Miami Beach: Highest ADR in the state. Strict regulations in some areas. Verify zoning carefully.
  • Tampa Bay STR: Moderate STR market. Growing event-driven demand. Lower ADR than beach markets.
  • Cape Canaveral / Cocoa Beach: Space Coast launches drive demand spikes. Emerging STR market.

STR Loan Requirements in Florida

Florida STR DSCR loan requirements differ slightly from standard DSCR:

  • Down payment: 25% minimum (vs. 20% for long-term rentals)
  • Credit score: 620+ minimum, 680+ recommended for best rates
  • Rate premium: Typically 0.25-0.50% above standard DSCR rates
  • HOA verification: Must confirm HOA permits STR before application
  • Zoning verification: Must confirm local ordinances permit STR
  • Property type: Single-family, condos (HOA permitting), townhomes
  • Income documentation: 12 months of STR income OR market rent appraisal

Critical Pre-Purchase Checks for Florida STR

Before purchasing any Florida property for STR, verify: (1) HOA rules explicitly permit short-term rentals, (2) local municipality permits STR in that zoning district, (3) the county or city registration/licensing requirements, and (4) whether the property is in a zone that has imposed or is considering STR restrictions. Many Florida communities have restricted STR since 2020.
