Common investor questions

Do I need experience to get an investor loan?

Many investor loan programs allow first-time investors depending on the property, structure, and overall scenario. Experience helps, but is not always required.

Do I need W-2 income to qualify?

Some programs rely more on property income and deal structure than traditional income documentation. DSCR loans, for example, qualify based on the property's cash flow rather than personal income.

How much down payment is required?

That depends on strategy, property type, and loan path. The goal is to review the deal and determine what is realistic based on your specific scenario.

What types of properties do you work with?

Single family, duplexes, small multifamily, condos, townhomes, and mixed-use properties. Fix & flip, rentals, and refinances all fit within the advisory model.

How long does the review process take?

Initial deal review is typically quick. The more complete the information submitted, the faster the scenario can be assessed and moved toward a next step.
