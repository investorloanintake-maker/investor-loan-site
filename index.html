FAQ

Common investor questions

Do I need W-2 income or tax returns?

No. Business purpose loans qualify based on the property and deal structure — not your personal income. No W-2s, no tax returns, no personal debt-to-income calculation.

Do I need an LLC to get a business purpose loan?

Most BPL programs require the property to be purchased in a business entity. We can walk you through how to structure this if you haven't already.

How fast can you actually close?

Our average is 10 business days from completed application to close. Some deals close faster depending on title and property condition. We'll give you a realistic timeline upfront.

Do you lend nationwide?

Yes. Our lender network covers all 50 states. Florida and Ohio are markets we know especially well.

What's the minimum credit score?

Most programs start at 660. Some go lower depending on LTV and deal strength. Credit score matters less here than it does with conventional financing.

Can first-time investors get these loans?

Yes. Many BPL programs don't require prior investment experience. The deal and the numbers matter more than your track record.
