Privacy Policy

How Viador Partners collects, uses, and protects your information.

Effective Date: March 1, 2026

Viador Partners LLC ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal information you provide through our forms — including your name, email address, phone number, and property or deal details.
  • Website analytics data collected automatically through Google Analytics, including pages visited, session duration, device type, and general geographic location.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Review and analyze your deal submissions
  • Follow up with you regarding financing options
  • Improve our services and website experience

HubSpot CRM

We use HubSpot as our customer relationship management platform. Your information is stored securely in HubSpot's systems. HubSpot maintains its own privacy practices, which you can review at hubspot.com/privacy-policy.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share deal details with lending partners solely to obtain rate quotes for your specific transaction. These partners receive only the information necessary to evaluate your deal.

Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies from the following services:

  • Google Analytics — to understand website traffic, user behavior, and improve site performance.
  • HubSpot tracking — for lead management and to personalize your experience on our site.

Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. We will respond to all requests in a timely manner.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your personal data, please contact us:

Email: chad@viadorpartners.com
Phone: (813) 419-2983

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
