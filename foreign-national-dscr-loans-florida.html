Foreign National DSCR — Florida

Foreign National DSCR Loans in Florida

Florida is one of the most active markets for international real estate investors — and DSCR loans let foreign nationals finance Florida rentals by qualifying on the property's rental income, typically without US tax returns or US credit. Expect a larger down payment, passport and entry documentation, source-of-funds, and often US LLC vesting; FIRPTA is handled at closing. A fit to explore if you…

  • Are a non-US citizen buying a Florida rental or short-term rental.
  • Have limited or no US tax history or US credit.
  • Can document source-of-funds and a larger down payment.
  • Want to hold the Florida property in a US LLC.

Maybe not the best fit if you…

  • Are buying a Florida home to live in (DSCR is for investment property).
  • Can't document source-of-funds.
  • Are a US citizen/resident with full income docs.

Why Florida, and how it qualifies

Florida draws significant international investment for its rental demand, tourism and short-term-rental markets, and accessibility to Latin American, Canadian, and European buyers. That rental demand is exactly what a DSCR loan is qualified on: whether the property's rent supports its housing payment, rather than the borrower's personal income. Frequently Asked Questions

Can a foreign national buy Florida investment property with a DSCR loan?

Yes. DSCR programs are widely used by international investors buying Florida rentals, qualifying on the property's rental income rather than US tax returns or US credit. Programs commonly require a larger down payment, passport and entry documentation, and source-of-funds, and many allow US LLC vesting. Requirements vary by lender.

Why is Florida popular with foreign national investors?

Florida draws significant international investment for its rental demand, tourism and short-term-rental markets, and accessibility to Latin American, Canadian, and European buyers. That demand is the property-income side of a DSCR loan, which is what the loan is qualified on.
