What is a Business Purpose Loan (BPL)?+
A Business Purpose Loan is a real estate loan made to a business entity — typically an LLC — for an investment property. Underwritten on the deal and asset, not your personal income. This removes property count limits and income documentation requirements.
How is BPL different from a DSCR loan?+
DSCR is a type of BPL — it's a specific underwriting method. BPL is the broader framework, which also covers fix & flip bridge loans, blanket portfolio loans, and other structures.
Do I need an LLC to get a BPL loan?+
Yes. Business Purpose Loans require a business entity as the borrower — most commonly an LLC. Setup is straightforward and typically under $200 in Florida or Ohio.
How long does a deal review take?+
Most reviews within 24 hours. Experienced investors with complete deal details are often reviewed the same business day.
